What Gallup Does

Gallup is most well-known for their polls: they conduct these surveys worldwide and the results are used to report on everything from global trends to changes in an individual workplace. More relevant to me, they have their own software and digital infrastructure that is used to create, host, and analyze the forms.

However, these polls are not actually explicitly a part of the company's 'job description' as it is; rather, Gallup is hired to provide advice to other companies and organizations about what they can do to retain talent, improve their workplace culture, and attract new employees-- 'Workplace Advisement' as they put it.

It was in pursuit of providing more accurate advice that Gallup first started polling people (and much of their surveys are still only conducted internally at client companies) but as they grew the yearly World Poll became a bigger and bigger thing.
