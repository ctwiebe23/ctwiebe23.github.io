Introduction

Hello! My name is Carston Wiebe, and I am a Sophomore here at UNL who majors in Computer Engineering.

This summer I got to stay at Creighton University in Omaha in order to intern at Gallup's nearby campus. The internship started May 20th (the Monday after school got out for the summer at UNL) and lasted until August 8th, running most of the summer. In that time I learned many valuable skills had a great experience learning from industry professionals about what we do in the field and how we do it.

As a part of my role was building websites from scratch, I figured that doing something similar would be perfect for my final project in this class. While many of the tools and infrastructure used to build sites at Gallup stayed at Gallup, I was still able to use what I learned to build this one entirely on my own-- no templates!
